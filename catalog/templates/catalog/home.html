About our School

we are one of the best, most diverse secondary schools in the uganda. Bulucheke was ranked among the nine four-star schools in uganda accrding to the 2009 ministry of education and sports ranking of private secondary school.

we provide a carefully assembled package that includes the academics,co-curricular and the christian social life well guided by our qualified staff. our christian and result-oriened approach to issues has proved appropriate.
